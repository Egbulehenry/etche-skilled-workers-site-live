Updates on Etche Skilled Workers

Track the progress and milestones of trained workers in the program

Skills Certification Ceremony Announced

The inaugural group of Etche skilled trainees has successfully completed the program and received their certifications in various fields...The Etche Skilled Worker program has announced its first skills certification ceremony for trainees who completed the 6-month training period.

Happy Etche Skilled Worker Excited For Getting A job On The Platform

The government and private sector are considering job placement schemes to link these skilled workers with employment opportunities. A database of certified artisans is also being compiled for local and regional job matching. Graduates from the Etche Skilled Worker initiative are now entering the workforce with recognized qualifications and real-world readiness.

New Courses Added to the Program

Two new courses — Welding & Fabrication and Solar Panel Installation — have been added to expand training options for interested applicants.

Refresher Training Begins Next Week

Refresher training for previously certified skilled workers begins next week at various training centers across the Etche region.
